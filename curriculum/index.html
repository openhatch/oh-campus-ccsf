2:30 pm to 3:30 / Contributing

The contributions workshop is a time for you to start contributing to open source projects. You have several options for how to contribute. You can learn about specific kinds of tasks that are especially good for newcomers and complete those tasks for open source projects in the Newcomer Tasks section. You can work with mentors from Participating Projects who are available, either in person or remotely, to help you contribute to their project. Or you can work on whatever project you want, however you want in the Freestyle group.

Make sure to go to move to the right spot in the room!

Following Up

Want to stay involved in the free and open source community? You can:

  • stay involved with OpenHatch by joining us on IRC and on our mailing lists
  • join your school's $opensourcecommunity
  • apply for one of many open source internships

Also, don't forget to take our exit survey and help us improve future events.
